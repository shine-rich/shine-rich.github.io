CareBridge

A self-contained, school-ready mental health support solution — featuring real-time chat, AI-assisted planning, and secure offline operation.

  • Student Chat UI: Real-time CBT support via secure WebSocket
  • Counselor Dashboard: Review transcripts, generate treatment plans with AI
  • CareBridge Local LLM: On-device private language model for summaries and forms
  • Study Pal: Between-session support tool for students to review plans
  • AI Guardian: Monitors network activity and LLM box integrity
  • Built-in VPN: WireGuard-powered remote connection support
